# MyMeetings

MyMeetings is a comprehensive meeting management and QR code scanning Android application designed to bridge the gap between desktop calendar invites and your mobile device. 

## 🚀 Overview

The MyMeetings ecosystem consists of two main components:
1. **The Android Application (This Repository)**: A modern, sleek Android app built with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, and Material 3. It serves as a central hub for managing your upcoming meetings, securely storing them locally using an encrypted Room Database.
2. **The PC Companion App ([ICS2QR](https://github.com/kalaiselvan-arumugam/ICS2QR))**: A standalone JavaFX desktop application that runs in your system tray. It accepts bulky Microsoft Teams `.ics` calendar invites, heavily minifies them, and generates compact QR codes.

## ✨ Features

* **Advanced QR Scanner**: Integrated with ML Kit to scan the highly compressed QR codes generated by the PC app. It seamlessly decompresses the payload (handling Gzip and Base64) to extract meeting details.
* **Meeting Dashboard**: A beautiful, dark-themed dashboard that lists all your upcoming meetings, complete with Teams logos and quick-join buttons.
* **Control Center Quick Settings Tile**: Includes an Android Quick Settings Tile, allowing you to launch the QR scanner instantly from anywhere on your phone by pulling down your notification shade.
* **Secure Local Storage**: All meetings are stored locally on your device using a Room Database with SQLCipher encryption, ensuring your calendar data remains private.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* **Kotlin**
* **Jetpack Compose** (UI)
* **CameraX & ML Kit** (QR Scanning)
* **Room Database & SQLCipher** (Encrypted Local Storage)
* **Coroutines & Flow** (Asynchronous operations)
* **Hilt** (Dependency Injection)

## 📱 How to Use the Ecosystem

### Step 1: Set up the PC Companion App
1. Download or clone the PC app from its repository: [ICS2QR on GitHub](https://github.com/kalaiselvan-arumugam/ICS2QR)
2. Run the `MyMeetings.jar` on your desktop. It will quietly dock in your system tray.
3. Whenever you receive a Microsoft Teams calendar invite (`.ics` file), simply drag and drop it into the PC app window.
4. The PC app will instantly extract the essential details, compress the data, and display a QR code on your screen.

### Step 2: Use the Android App
1. Install and open the **MyMeetings** app on your Android device.
2. From the main dashboard, tap the **Scan** button (or use the Quick Settings Tile from your notification shade).
3. Point your camera at the QR code displayed on your PC screen.
4. The Android app will instantly read the QR code, decompress the data, and save the meeting details.
5. The meeting will now appear on your dashboard! When the time comes, just tap **Join** to open Microsoft Teams.

## 📦 Building the Android App

This project uses Gradle. To build the application:
1. Open the project in Android Studio.
2. Sync the Gradle files.
3. Build and run the `app` module on your emulator or physical device.
